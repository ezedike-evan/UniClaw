# Events
_Last updated: 2026-04-17_
_Source: UNILAG SUG / faculty press bodies / @unilagofficial on social_

## Recurring annual events

- **Matriculation Ceremony** — welcomes fresh students; usually early in the academic session.
- **Convocation** — graduation ceremony, typically held around January/February. [VERIFY]
- **Inter-Faculty Games** — sporting competition between all faculties, Main Bowl.
- **SUG Cultural Week** — music, pageantry, cultural displays.
- **Founder's Day** — commemorates UNILAG's founding in 1962.
- **TEDxUnilag** — student-organised TEDx event, dates announced by the organising team.
- **JCI UNILAG Week** — leadership and enterprise programmes.
- **Faculty Week / "XXX Week"** — each faculty hosts a themed week (e.g., Engineering Week, Law Week).

## Popular student organisations
- **Students' Union Government (SUG)**
- **National Association of Nigerian Students (NANS)**
- Faculty and departmental associations (e.g., NULAS for Law, NUESA for Engineering)
- Religious fellowships (NIFES, MSSN, FOCUS, etc.)
- Debate, drama, music, and chess clubs

## FAQs
Q: Where do big events usually happen?
A: J. F. Ade Ajayi Auditorium, the Main Auditorium, the Main Bowl (for sports), and Tayo Aderinokun Lecture Theatre.

Q: How do I get my event listed on UniClaw?
A: Use `/contribute` in the bot or the Contribute tab in the Mini App, category "events".
