## FAQs
Q: I need my transcript urgently.
A: Apply through the Registrar's Academic Records office, pay the prescribed fee, and follow up in person. Processing takes weeks, not days.

Q: Who handles sexual harassment reports?
A: The UNILAG Sexual Harassment Response Team. Reports can be made confidentially — ask Student Affairs or Counselling for the current contact. [VERIFY]
